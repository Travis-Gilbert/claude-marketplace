## Part 1: Introduction (5 minutes)

Read the following to the participant:

> Thank you for taking the time to participate today. My name is [Moderator Name], and I will be guiding you through this session.
>
> We are testing [the product/feature], not you. There are no right or wrong answers, and you cannot make any mistakes. Your honest feedback is incredibly valuable and will help us improve the experience.
>
> The session will take approximately [45/60] minutes. Here is what will happen:
>
> 1. I will ask you a few background questions.
> 2. Then I will give you some tasks to complete using [the product/prototype].
> 3. After the tasks, I will ask you some follow-up questions.
>
> **Think aloud:** As you work through the tasks, please share your thoughts out loud. Tell me what you are looking at, what you are thinking, what you expect to happen, and what confuses you. If you go quiet, I may remind you to keep talking.
>
> **Recording consent:** With your permission, we would like to record this session (screen and audio) for our team's reference. The recording will only be used internally and will not be shared publicly. Is that okay with you?

- [ ] Participant gave verbal consent to recording
- [ ] Recording started

> Do you have any questions before we begin?
